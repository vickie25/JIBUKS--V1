### **VAT Rate: 16%**

### **VAT on Sales (Output VAT):**
```
Sale Amount: KES 10,000
VAT (16%): KES 1,600
Total: KES 11,600

Journal Entry:
Debit: Accounts Receivable - KES 11,600
Credit: Sales Revenue - KES 10,000
Credit: VAT Payable - KES 1,600
```

### **VAT on Purchases (Input VAT):**
```
Purchase Amount: KES 5,000
VAT (16%): KES 800
Total: KES 5,800

Journal Entry:
Debit: Purchases/Expense - KES 5,000
Debit: VAT Receivable - KES 800
Credit: Accounts Payable - KES 5,800
```

### **VAT Return Calculation:**
